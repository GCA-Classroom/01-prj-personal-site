Volunteer Experience — buildOn
As a dedicated member of buildOn, I participated in a two-year service-learning program focused on global education equity. In both my junior and senior years of high school, I fundraised over $4,000 annually to support the construction of schools in underserved communities abroad.

In Summer 2023, I traveled to rural Nicaragua, where I lived with a host family and helped lay the foundation for a new school. A year later, I joined a similar trek to Babel2, Senegal, continuing hands-on work while deeply engaging with local communities. Despite language barriers, I built meaningful relationships rooted in shared purpose and human connection.

These experiences taught me resilience, cultural humility, and the power of education as a catalyst for change—fueling my long-term passion for service, youth empowerment, and global health equity.
